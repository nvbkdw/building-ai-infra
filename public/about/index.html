About

A quick look at my journey building ML infrastructure and the ethos behind this blog.

I’m Ruoyu Huang, an engineer obsessed with making AI infrastructure boring—in the very best way. I have spent the last several years building systems that keep models trained, evaluated, deployed, and cost-efficient for teams shipping real products.

  • Battle-tested patterns I leaned on while scaling teams: ruthless observability, developer-centric tooling, and principled experimentation.
  • Candid notes on what actually breaks in production and the pragmatic trade-offs we make to ship.
